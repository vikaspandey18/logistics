Cargo Services

Cargo refers to the goods or products that are being transported from one location to another.

It include a wide range of items, such as packages, bulk goods, hazardous materials, and more.

Cargo is an essential part of the transport industry, as it is the primary reason for transporting goods from one location to another. There are several different types of cargo that may be transported, including:

  • General Cargo.
  • Bulk Cargo.
  • Hazardous Materials
  • Hazardous Materials
  • Perishable Goods
  • Oversized Cargo

Transport websites may offer a variety of services related to cargo transportation, such as tracking and tracing, freight forwarding, and customs clearance. These services help to ensure that cargo is transported safely and efficiently from one location to another.
