At Logistics, we take our users' privacy seriously and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you when you use our services, including:

  • Your name, address, and contact information
  • Your billing and payment information
  • Information about the goods or services you purchase or use
  • Information about the goods or services you purchase or use
  • Information you provide in communications with us

We may also collect information automatically when you use our services, such as your IP address, device information, and browsing activity.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to provide and improve our services, including

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Communicating with you about your orders or our services

  • Providing personalized recommendations and marketing
  • Complying with legal or regulatory requirements/li>

We may also use your information for other purposes with your consent.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use industry-standard security technologies and procedures to safeguard your information.

However, no security system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information.

Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our services, such as shipping carriers, payment processors, or marketing agencies.

We may also share your information in response to a legal request or if we believe that it is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety or that of our users or others.

Your Rights and Choices

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to or restrict our use of your information. You may also have the right to receive a copy of your information in a machine-readable format.

To exercise your rights or to request more information, please contact us at info@logistics.com.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website or by other means.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@logistics.com.
